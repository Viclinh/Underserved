About Us

We provide services that help underserved communities to get access to better healthcare. Our services include interactive map for searching doctors and an Azure Chat Bot that can search for doctors, provide resources for health issues, and provide explanation about medicine. We also provide services for underserved communities patients to connect with healthcare consultants online face to face as well as places to them to post their success recovering photos.
